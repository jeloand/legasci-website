About Legazpi City Science High School

The year 2004 marks the beginning of an institutional story that has been etched in the history of Legazpi City, the Legazpi City Science High School saga.

Fathered by the City Mayor of Legazpi, Honorable Mayor Noel E. Rosal, the the Legazpi City Science High School takes pride in its gradual but steady growth as an academic institution, firmly grounded on the principles of commitment, service, innovativeness, and transformation.

Educational Principles


  • Commitment

  • Service

  • Innovation

  • Transformation

The Legazpi City Science High School commits itsef to the provision of a completely-responsive basic high school education through its Junior and Senor High School curricula. With its two academic track and one technical and vocational livelihood track strands in place--Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM), Accountancy, Business and Management (ABM), and Information and Communications Technology (ICT)--Legasci endeavors to entwine strong fibers of equitable, culture-sensitive, value-laden academic training and maximized life-skills development. It recognizes every student's potential for maximum growth and sustainable progress.


As a learner-centered public institutional servant, Legasci ensures its adherance to the nobility of educational service. Hence, it vows to create an enabling and encouraging learning environment to its students. Thus, it countinuously strives to improve itself to better serve the community.


To keep its pace abreast with the changing times, Legasci radiates an aura of dynamism in bringing about efficiently-designed and effectively-executed curricular programs. Purposively, it creates a learning environment that generates enthusiasm, creativity, and productivity.


Strongly advocating education as preparation for life , Legasci caters not only to the academic advancement of its students but also to their transformative maturation as members of the community. Such demand entails the optimal honing of the students' initiatives, capacities, and sensibilities.

It is Legazpi City Science High School, the home of our dreams till we reach our stars.

--Legasci Hymn

Mission

To protect and promote the right of every Filipino to quality, equitable, culture-based and complete basic education where students learn in a child-friendly, gender sensitive and safe and motivating environment.

Teachers facilitate learning and constantly nurture every learner. Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure an enabling and supportive environment for effective learning to happen.

Family, community and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners.

Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country in whose competencies and values enabled them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation.

We are learner-centered public institution, the Department of Education continuously improves itself to better serve its stakeholders.
